How It Works

Calibration → Signal → Alert

No sentiment. No gut feel. Edge is calculated from historical settlement data and compared to current market prices.

01
Calibration model
Win rates at each price level are measured from 80,000+ settled Kalshi markets. The model knows what a 75¢ market actually resolves YES at historically — not what the price implies.
02
Live market scan
Edge Alert polls active Kalshi markets continuously — crypto 15-minute windows and late-game sports markets. Only scans when conditions match calibrated edges (final 3 minutes, 85%+ game completion).
03
Edge calculation
For each live market, edge = (calibrated win rate × payout) − (loss probability × stake) − Kalshi taker fee. Only signals with positive edge after fees are surfaced.
04
Telegram alert
Qualifying signals are pushed to your private Telegram channel instantly. Each alert shows the ticker, side, price, edge %, expected value, and confidence tier. No noise — just actionable signals.

FAQ

Common questions

What is Edge Alert?
Edge Alert is a signal monitoring service for Kalshi prediction markets. It scans active markets, applies a calibration model built on 80,000+ historical settlements, and alerts you when the current market price diverges from the calibrated win rate — creating a potential edge net of fees.
Is this investment advice?
No. Edge Alert is an informational and data service. Signals are data outputs — not recommendations to buy or sell any contract. Prediction markets carry risk. You make all trading decisions. Past signal accuracy does not guarantee future results.
Which markets does it cover?
Currently: Kalshi BTC 15-minute price direction markets (KXBTC15M series) and in-game sports markets for NBA, NHL, and NCAAMB. Sports signals only trigger when games are 85%+ complete, where calibrated edges are strongest.
How fast are alerts delivered?
Signals are delivered via Telegram within ~60 seconds of being detected. The scanner runs continuously and pushes alerts immediately when a qualifying signal appears. No batching — each signal is sent as it's found.
How is edge calculated?
Edge = (calibrated win rate × payout) − (loss probability × stake) − Kalshi taker fee. The calibrated win rate comes from historical settlement data at each price bucket. Only signals where this calculation is positive after fees are surfaced.
Do I need a Kalshi account?
Yes, if you want to trade on the signals. You'll need a Kalshi account and available balance. Edge Alert only provides the signals — you place your own trades. Edge Alert does not have access to your Kalshi account.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Subscriptions are month-to-month with no lock-in. Cancel from your Stripe billing portal at any time. Access continues through the end of your billing period.
